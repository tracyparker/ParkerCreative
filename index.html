What We Do!

Responsive

We live in a world on the go! Responsive web design ensures that your site keeps up with your customers, no matter what device they use to surf the web. From desktops, to tablets, to mobile phones...we have you covered!

Research

Need a little help with your research? Want to better understand your target demographics but don't know where to start? Let us help. We are well trained in the field of internet research and can give you the data you need to better understand your customers.

Photography

Need some shots of your products for your online store? Would you like to give your clients a virtual tour of your location? Does your band need concert photos to draw more fans to the site? We have shutterbugs on staff to give your site the quality photos it deserves.

Our Code Louisville Roots!

Parker Creative was founded by Susan "Tracy" Parker, after losing her job at a local factory for medical reasons. She heard about CODE LOUISVILLE, and began taking coding classes. Less than a year later she found herself launching an app startup called MixxThis, with Parker Creative soon to follow. We are big fans of the Code Louisville program here at Parker Creative since our founder, and most of our staff, are graduates of the program. Want to learn to code?
